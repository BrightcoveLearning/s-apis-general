Mass ingestion/migration

If you are migrating videos to Video Cloud from another platform, or just have a large number of videos you need to ingest, let us help you make the process smooth by following these guidelines:

  • Open a Support ticket at least 3 days in advance indicating that you are planning to start ingesting a large amount of assets.
  • Provide a timeline of when you expect to start and finish.
  • Indicate which ingestion method are you planning to use: API, MRSS, Aspera (with our without XML manifest).
  • Indicate the total number of videos you will ingest.
  • Indicate the average video file size.
  • Indicate the average video duration (minutes).
  • Indicate the region where you content is located.
Note: each XML manifest can contain up to 20 video assets.
